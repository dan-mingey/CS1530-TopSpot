    Friends Help

    The top of the Friends page contains a navigation bar at the top. The first element is a "Help" button (which lead you to this page). The button to the right of the Help button (circled above in red) leads back to the select floor page which lets you choose a floor to view. Clicking on this button will take you there.

    The next button in the navigation bar allows you to choose to unclaim the seat you are currently sitting in if you wish to do so. If you do not have a spot claimed, clicking this button will make you aware of that. Otherwise, clicking this button will allow you to unclaim the spot you are sitting at.

    The right-most element at the top of the navigation bar is a logout button. Clicking on this button will log you out of the TopSpot. You will be prompted to login again if you wish to enter the application again.

    Below the text "Friends List" is your list of friends. If you do not have any friends, there will be text displayed notifying you that you do not have any friends in TopSpot at the moment.

    If you wish to remove a friend, click the "Remove Friend" button under the name of the friend you wish to remove. These buttons are highlighted above in red. If you select this button for a particular user, you two will no longer be friends and they will be removed from your friends list.

    If you would like to add a friend to your friends list, you may do so by typing in their username in the text box labeled "Enter Username" and then by clicking the button to the right of this box labeled "Add Friend". This is highlighted above in red. If an invalid name is entered, you will be notifyed in an alert and you can try to enter a valid username again.

    Above is an example of adding a friend. First type in the username and click "Add Friend".

    If a valid username was entered, the username of the new friend will be displayed in your friends list.

    We hope this was helpful. Please hit the "Back" button in the top bar of the screen to go back to the Friends page!
