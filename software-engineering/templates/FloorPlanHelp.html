    Floor Plan Help

    Once you select a floor, you will be taken to a map of the floor plan. If you see a green square that means that you are sitting in that spot.

    Once you select a floor, you will be taken to a map of the floor plan. If you see a green square that means that you are sitting in that spot.

    In the top navigation bar next to the help button, you have the option to select a new floor. If you wish to view the aviailable seats on a differnt floor, click this button and you will go back to the floor select page.

    If you currently have a spot claimed, you have the option of clicking the "Unclaim Spot" button in the navigation floor. Selecting the "Unclaim Spot" button will result in a prompt asking you are sure you wish to give up your spot. Selecting ok will free up your spot and you will no longer have a spot claimed. If you select this button and you do not have a claimed spot, a prompt will appear notifying you that you have no spots to unclaim.

    Spots with a yellow outline indicate that that seat has an outlet available.

    If you wish to select a new seat to sit at, click on the seat you wish to sit in (indicated by the red arrow). A prompt will then appear asking you to confirm your seat selection.

    The newly selected seat will now turn green and the spot will be reserved for yourself.

    We hope this was helpful. Please hit the "Back" button in the top bar of the screen to go back to the Floorplan page!
